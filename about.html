About Me

I’m Nipun Wimalasooriya, A freelancer from Kurunegala Sri Lanka. I studied my primary education at Kavisigamuwa Primary college and then shifted to St. Annes College Kurunegala. Currently I am studing at the department of Computing and Information Systems at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. I've worked as a freelancer for 5 years and have experiences in Logo Designing, Graphic Designing, Video Editing, Web Development and Animation. I also have worked in Kassa Advertising private limited as a graphic designer.
